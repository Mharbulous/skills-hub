## Downloading receipts {#downloading-receipts}

**Receipt availability:** The portal has downloadable receipts for all invoices
back to January 2019.

**Receipt content:** Each receipt shows invoice number, date, description, and
amount with GST breakdown. Receipts download as PDFs.

**Naming convention for filing:**
`DATE | INV# | LSBC | Description | Amount.pdf`

## Key facts {#key-facts}

**E&O insurance = the "Indemnity Fee" component of LSBC annual fees.** It is NOT
a CBIA/Lawyers Financial product. The annual fee invoice breaks down into:
- Practice Fee
- Indemnity Fee (this is the E&O insurance)
- GST

**CBIA / Lawyers Financial** is a separate personal life insurance policy — not
related to LSBC.

**Trust Reporting Fees** are separate invoices from annual fees. Late filing incurs
a $400 fee (+ GST).

**Multiple payments per year:** LSBC typically has 2+ invoices per year (annual fee
+ second instalment + trust reporting fees), so expect multiple receipts.
