## Scale Values (s. 3 of Appendix B)

| Scale | Per Unit | When Used |
|-------|----------|-----------|
| A | $60.00 | Matters of little or less than ordinary difficulty |
| B | $110.00 | Matters of ordinary difficulty (default if no scale fixed or agreed) |
| C | $170.00 | Matters of more than ordinary difficulty |

**Notes on scale:**
- If no scale is fixed or agreed to, costs are assessed under Scale B (s. 2(4)).
- On acceptance of a Rule 9-1 offer to settle, costs are assessed under Scale B (s. 2(8)).
- Court may order 1.5× unit value if award at the fixed scale would be grossly inadequate or unjust due to unusual circumstances (s. 2(5)).

## Daily Rate Rules (s. 4)

- If time spent during a day is **2.5 hours or less** → allow **1/2** the units for that day.
- If time spent during a day is **more than 5 hours** → increase units by **1/2**.
- For preparation items: if the attendance itself is 2.5 hours or less → allow **1/2** the prep units.
- Registrar may allow prep units for an activity that does not take place or is adjourned, up to the maximum for one day.

## Common Disbursements

Out-of-pocket expenses recoverable in addition to tariff costs. Each must be reasonable and necessarily incurred. Disputed disbursements require attached invoices and receipts showing payment.

- Court filing fees
- Process server / personal service fees
- Photocopying / printing costs (court-ordered or for service)
- Expert report fees
- Transcript fees (examination for discovery, trial)
- Witness fees and conduct money
- Travel expenses (if out-of-town attendance required — see also Item 48)
- Mediation fees (mediator's share)
- Search fees (land title, corporate registry, etc.)
- Courier / delivery charges
- Long distance telephone / fax charges
- Postage for registered mail service
